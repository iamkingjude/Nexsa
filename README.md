# Nexsa

> The safest entry point into Web3.

Nexsa is an open-source platform that helps beginners navigate Web3 without getting wrecked  by detecting scams, verifying projects,
and delivering trustworthy education before money moves.


## The Problem

Every year, millions of people step into Web3 and immediately face the same landmines:

- Wallet confusion:  wrong setup, seed phrase mistakes, lost funds
- Scam projects: rug pulls and honeypots disguised as opportunities
- Phishing attacks: fake sites stealing credentials
- Fake airdrops: social engineering dressed as free money
- No guidance: onboarding docs written for developers, not beginners

Most people don't need another DeFi protocol. They need a system that keeps them safe before they lose anything.


## What Nexsa Does

### Phase 1  Safety Tools *(In Development)*

Scam Checker | Paste a token address, link, or project name. Get an instant risk report.

Project Verifier | Cross-checks contracts, socials, and on-chain data against red flags.

Beginner Guides | Short, jargon-free breakdowns of wallets, keys, gas, and more.

### Phase 2  Intelligence Layer
- AI Web3 Assistant: Ask anything. Get honest, context-aware answers.
- Wallet Risk Analyzer:  Audit wallet exposure and approval risks.
- Smart Contract Scanner:  Flag dangerous patterns before you sign.

### Phase 3  Community & Reputation
-  Community Reputation Layer:  User-verified project signals.
-  Personalized Learning Paths:  Education that adapts to your experience level.
-  Web3 Safety Score:  Your on-chain safety rating across wallets and history.

## Why Nexsa?

There are security tools for experienced users. There are education platforms for curious learners. There's almost nothing built specifically for the moment someone opens their first wallet and doesn't know what they don't know.

Nexsa is built for that moment.
